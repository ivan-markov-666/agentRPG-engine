# Default Area: Village Square

## Description
A small hub where villagers gather to trade news, rest, and seek help from the elder. It is a stable anchor point for intro sessions.

## Points of interest
- The stone well that feeds the settlement.
- Community notice board pinned with new adventuring hooks.

## Connections
- Forest outskirts trail used for hunting quests.
- Training grounds on the hill for combat drills.

## Notes
- NPCs: Elder (quest giver)
- Features: Well, notice board
- Safety: Safe zone, starting point
- Hooks: Market gossip, quest postings

## Conditions
- Reputation Neutral or higher with the militia.
- No active hostile encounters inside the square (sanctuary rule).

## Threats
- If wolves breach the market, merchants double prices.
- Rival adventurers may claim quests if the square is ignored for two sessions.
