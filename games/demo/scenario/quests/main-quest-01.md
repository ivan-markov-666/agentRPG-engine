# Prove Your Worth

## Summary
Earn the village's trust by helping the elder and securing the outskirts.

## Story
The elder reveals that strange howls echo from the forest while the training grounds remain short-staffed. The party must alternate between defending the villagers and scouting the old watchtower to ensure peace returns to the settlement.

## Steps
1. Speak with the elder in the [[default-area]] village square.
2. Clear the wolves near the forest edge.
3. Scout the abandoned watchtower for remaining threats.
4. Report back to the elder for your reward.

## Rewards
- 150 XP
- 50 gold pieces
- Reputation boost with the village

## Hooks
- Visiting the [[default-area]] elder unlocks access to the militia quartermaster.
- Clearing the wolves uncovers rumors about the [[training-grounds]] sabotage.

## Encounters
- Wolf pack ambush near the forest edge.
- Watchtower specter guarding the final clue.

## Notes
- NPCs: Elder Maros (quest giver) and militia sergeant Isla at the training grounds.
- Hooks: Success unlocks access to advanced training sessions and merchant discounts.

## Aftermath
- Follow-up quest: Track the saboteurs into the northern woods.
- Merchants offer rare gear if the party maintains patrols around the square.

## Outcome Hooks
- [[default-area]] NPC response: Elder Maros now trusts the party with diplomacy to nearby towns.
- [[training-grounds]] escalation hook: Sergeant Isla requests elite drills against the saboteurs' leader.

## Conditions
- Reputation friendly with the village guard.
- Complete within two in-game days before more wolves arrive.

## Fail State
- Merchants increase prices by 20%.
- Rival squad claims the reward and blocks future access.

## Outcome
- The militia regains control of the outskirts and unlocks advanced drills.
- The elder publicly backs the party, increasing their status in the village.

## Aftermath
- Follow-up quest: Track the saboteurs into the northern woods.
- Merchants offer rare gear if the party maintains patrols around the square.
